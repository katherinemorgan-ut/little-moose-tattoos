Frequently Asked Questions



Do you accept walk-ins?

You betcha! However, we won't be able to gaurantee availability if you don't make an appointment.


How can I schedule an appointment?

You can call or email the shop and we will get you scheduled. You can also scheduled an appointment in the website!


How can I prepare for my appointment?

Make sure you drink a lot of water and are well-rested. You should make sure to eat a meal before your appointment as well. If your skin is sunburned, cut/scratched/scraped or shows signs of an allergic reaction, we likely will ask you to reschedule. Please don't take Tylenol or other acetaminophen drugs before your appointment. Make sure to bring your ID, and anything else to make you feel comfortable during your appointment. Some customers prefer to bring blankets, pillows, tablets or laptops to help keep comfortable during the appointment.


What are your sanitary precautions?

We are extremely serious about sanitary procedures! We use single-use, disposable tattoo supplies and hospital-grade disinfectant on all surfaces. After every session, we perform a full deep clean of the artist's station and the shop is cleaned daily. All of our staff are trained in bloodborne pathogens and cross-contamination.
